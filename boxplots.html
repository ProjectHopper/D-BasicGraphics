{Boxplots}
  • A boxplot provides a graphical view of the median, quartiles, maximum, and minimum of a data set.
  • Titles and axes labels can be added to the boxplot.
  • A boxplot may also indicate which observations, if any, might be considered outliers. These are indicated by small circles. 

	
	>#Generate 25 random numbers.
	>xdat = rnorm(25)
	>
	>boxplot(xdat,main='boxplot name',
	ylab='label of y-axis',
	xlab='label of x-axis')
{Boxplots: Orientation} The default orientation of the boxplot is vertical. A horizontally orientation can be specified by setting the `horizontal' parameter to TRUE. 

	
	boxplot(xdat,
	main='boxplot name',
	xlab='label of x-axis',
	horizontal=TRUE)
